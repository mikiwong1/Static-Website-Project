Spots in NYC

NYC is a diverse place with many exciting places to visit. In this page, we will be focusing on three main attractions located in Manhattan. There are many more exciting spots to see in all 5 boroughs which are not mentioned here. However, all the activities listed below you can visit for free and easily accesible.

Central Park

The first is Central Park. It is a free park where you can enjoy many scenic views that change throughout the seaasons. One of these activities are bike riding which the park can let you rent out bikes to your own use. There is also a pedicab tour in case you would rather sit back and relax while enjoying the view of nature. Walking tours are also a great option to get moving and get to take your time sightseeing. Since, Central Park is so big, there are many scenes to view and you can plan your trip there to more than one time.
